# Pika Prompt Pack

Use one consistent character reference image if possible. For Pika, start with 10-second, 16:9, 720p or 1080p when available. Avoid naming protected brands or copying a specific show. Use "anime-inspired preschool cartoon" instead.

## Shared Style Prompt

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon, soft rounded shapes, gentle expressive child character, warm friendly colors, clean bathroom/bedroom/home background, slow clear motion, calming mood, child-safe, no scary faces, no fast camera movement, no text on screen, no logos, no watermark, simple readable action, smooth hand and arm motion, soft lighting.

## Character Direction

The child starts upset and refusing with crossed arms or turned body. Over the clip, the child slowly relaxes, looks at the task, tries one tiny step, and ends calmer. Movement should be slow enough for a distressed preschool child to understand.

## Teeth

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child in a cozy bathroom refuses to brush teeth at first, arms crossed and frowning gently. A toothbrush and cup are nearby. The child takes one slow breath, reaches for the toothbrush, accepts help, and gently brushes front teeth with tiny circles. Slow clear hand movement, calm face by the end, warm colors, soft rounded shapes, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Bath

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child in a bright bathroom refuses bath time at first, sitting near a bathtub with arms crossed. Warm water, bubbles, and a soft towel are visible. The child slowly touches the water with one hand, relaxes, then washes one arm with soap bubbles. Very slow movement, clear water and soap interaction, calm ending, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Food

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child at a small table refuses dinner at first, turned slightly away from a plate. The plate has simple colorful food and a spoon. The child slowly looks, smells the food, touches the spoon, then takes one tiny bite and looks calmer. Gentle movement, no pressure, warm kitchen background, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Bedtime

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child resists bedtime in a cozy bedroom, arms crossed beside a bed. A soft blanket, pillow, and small night light are visible. The child slowly chooses the blanket, climbs into bed, and settles calmly. Slow breathing rhythm, warm night colors, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Clothes

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child refuses to get dressed at first, sitting near a shirt and socks. The child slowly looks at two clothing choices, touches one sock, and allows one foot to go in. Calm supportive mood, simple readable action, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Clean-up

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child refuses to clean up toys at first, standing near blocks and soft toys. The child slowly chooses one toy, picks it up, and places it in a bin. The room becomes slightly tidier. Slow clear arm movement, warm playful room, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Leaving

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child resists leaving a play area at first, standing with crossed arms. A caregiver hand is nearby but not pulling. The child slowly waves goodbye, takes three small steps toward the caregiver, and looks calmer. Gentle transition, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.

## Car Seat

Anime-inspired preschool cartoon. A young child resists sitting in a car seat at first, arms crossed beside an open car door. The child slowly climbs into the car seat, puts back against the seat, and accepts the buckle click. Calm safety-focused scene, no fast motion, no text, no logos, 10-second loop.
